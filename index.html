Super
- Soaker

The Super Soaker, a popular water gun brand, was invented in 1989 by engineer Lonnie Johnson. It uses pressurized air to shoot water with greater power and accuracy. Originally sold by Larami and now produced by Hasbro under the Nerf brand, Super Soaker has generated over $1 billion in sales. The first Super Soaker, went on sale in 1990. Lonnie Johnson's success with Super Soaker allowed him to establish his lab Johnson Research & Development Co., Inc.
